Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how RoadLoop (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the RoadLoop mobile application (the “App”). If you have questions, contact us at roadloop_help@outlook.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information: such as email address and basic profile details.
  • Location data: precise location (GPS), including background location while driving, used to detect and record journeys.
  • Journey data: trip history, distance, time, and routes.
  • Usage and diagnostics: app interactions and crash data to improve reliability.
  • Purchases: subscription status managed via Apple In-App Purchases.

How we use information

  • Provide journey tracking, mileage analytics, and driving insights.
  • Operate and improve the App.
  • Manage subscriptions and paid features.
  • Respond to support requests.

Sharing of information

We may share data with trusted service providers strictly to operate the App. We do not sell your personal information.

Location permissions

Location access is required for journey detection. You can change permissions at any time in your device settings. Some features may not work without location access.

Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data by contacting roadloop_help@outlook.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect changes.
