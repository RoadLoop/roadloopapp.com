What is RoadLoop?

RoadLoop is a driver-centric platform built around three pillars: Driver analytics, Vehicle profiles, and Segments. The goal is to make driving more informed, more organised, and more fun — while staying privacy-first.

1) Driver Profile & Mileage Analytics

RoadLoop automatically detects journeys and builds an understanding of your mileage and driving patterns. Each trip can be tagged with a confidence score, and you can delete trips you don’t want to keep.

2) Vehicle Profiles

Add your vehicles and keep them organised: odometer tracking, service history, and reminders for things like MOT/tax/insurance. The aim is to reduce admin and make “ownership hygiene” effortless.

3) Segments

Segments are the social/competitive layer: curated stretches of road you can compare performance on. We’re cautious with social features — we focus on visibility through segments and achievements rather than publishing full journeys.

Subscriptions

RoadLoop offers tiers that control segment usage and premium dashboards. Some dashboards and analytics are behind a paywall, and early “VIP” access may be offered for the first cohort of users.
